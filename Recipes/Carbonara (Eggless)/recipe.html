Carbonara (Eggless)

Ingredients:
* A box of noodles
* A tub of bologne
* A bag of shredded cheese
* Parmasean cheese
* Some form of cooking spray (recommended, but optional)

Utincels:
* Pan
* Spatula
* Some long spoon
* Pot

I'd recommend adding cooking spray to make clean up easier.

Dice balogne with butterknife (I use the Spatula instead so I don't have as much to clean up later).
Place in pan and durring this process, we will use spatula to flip over peices every now and again. (What it should look like when cooked fully down later).
Make sure that you keep a lookout for this while cooking noodles.

We are now going to boil the water for the noodles. Wait for the water to bubble up vigorously.

After the water starts boiling, add in the noodles and set timer for 9 minutes.
Stir every once and a while (maybe every 30 seconds to a minute).

Coming up to the diced balogne, it should look something like this when done

The ammount I cook is generally bigger than I can eat in one meal, so I put these ingredients in seperate containers for later use (optional).

Almost done, next we mix a bit of noodles and balogne (how ever much you think is filling) with cheese sprinkled on top for 1 minute.

Last, add some parmesean and we are done. This is a nice cheap meal that can last for about 3 meals.
