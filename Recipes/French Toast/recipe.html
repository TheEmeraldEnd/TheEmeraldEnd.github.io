French Toast

Ingredients:
* ½ Cap Vanilla
* 1 Cooking Spray
* 2 Eggs
* 4 Slices of Bread
* Pinch of Salt
* ½ Teaspoon Sugar (Not Pictured)
* 1 Cap Milk
* Syrup

Utincels:
* 1 Plate
* 1 Pan
* 1 Spatula
* 1 Container with Lid
* 2 Forks

Crask 2 eggs and scramble in container.

Add the ½ lid of vanilla to the mixture.

Add the ½ teaspoon of sugar.

Add the pinch of salt to the mixture.

Add the lid of milk to the mixture.

Now mix!!

Spray pan with cooking spray (not pictured) and set cooking on stove to 6.

Soak bread in mixture (both sides).

Cook until light brown.
This applies to both sides.

Repeat until the mixture is empty. For me, this equated to 4 pieces of french toast.

Add syrup and enjoy.
