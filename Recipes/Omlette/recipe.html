Omlette (4 eggs)

Ingredients:
* Eggs (I'll use 4)
* Salsa
* Cooking Spray
* Shredded Cheese

Utincels:
* Pan
* Spatula
* Cup
* Spoon (for salsa)
* Fork (For mixing eggs in Cup)

Step 1: Spray pan for easier cleaning.

Step 2: Crack eggs into cup.

Step 3: Wisk the eggs.

Step 4: Pour and wait for eggs in the pan.
The eggs should be done when sticky on top, but not liquidy.

Step 5: Add cheese.

Step 6: Fold.

Step 7: Put on plate and add more cheese (I like cheese).

Step 8: Add salsa and you are done. Drink with milk for good tasting breakfast.
