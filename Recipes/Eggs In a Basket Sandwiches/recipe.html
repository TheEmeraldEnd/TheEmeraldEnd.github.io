Eggs In a Basket Sandwiches

Note that I'm going to be using 3 of what is described here. This recipe describes cooking 1 eggs, but for the recipe I'm doing, tripple what is listed here.

Ingredients (for 1 sandwich):
* 2 bread slices
* 1 egg
* Salsa
* Cooking Spray (optional, but recommend)
* 1 Cheese slice

Utincels:
* Pan
* Spatula
* Spoon

Spray down pan before cooking. Recommend for better cleaning.

Put an egg sized hole in one of the slices of the bread.

Prepare the other bread by putting salsa and chease on the bread. This will make setting up easier.

Put bread into pan, then put eggs into the bread.
If you want them cooked fully, then wait until the back of egg is solid yellow before flipping over,
otherwise, this requires some practice as I like it runny and do it by feel.

After flipping, check to make sure the other side is cooked enough.
It will take some practice. I sometimes poke the center to see if it's good enough.
I like mine runny, so these are cooked with some liquid yolk in the middle.

Finish with putting the egg bread onto the other bread and you are finished.
This is a good and cheap meal with easy cleanup. It maybe has a 20-30 min cook and clean time.
