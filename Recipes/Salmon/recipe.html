Salmon

Ingredients:
* Salmon
* Seasoning Salt (Not sponsored [I used Lawry's but regular should be fine])

Utincels:
* 2 plates
* Tinfoil (not in picture)

Set in microwave with wrapping still in.

Put in microwave and then press defrost to level 1, then press 1.1 for quantity. This will put the time ammount for approximately to 10 min.

After done, (or maybe before) set to 400o fareignheight for 16 min.
I did mine maybe 1 min before the end of the microwave finishing.
Put in tin foil before starting (not shown in picture).

This is what the salman should look like after being taken out of the microwave. Don't eat yet, as it's still raw. Saltae both sides with the salt.

Once airfryer is done preheating, put in salmon and wait. (16 min may not be enough, but its a good starting point).

Finished salmon will look like this. Note the white coming out of the salmon (hopefully you put that tin foil I forgot to picture, but mentioned in text) and the flakey bits in the middle. Think 2 phone books with intertwining pages being ripped appart. That is what the salmon should feel like. Use a fork to cut a little slice in the middle to test. If it's still squishy like if it's still raw or has a big pink spot, then keep cooking. Check again a few min later and repeat.

This is the finished salmon. (I add another layer of the seasoning, but that's subjective).
